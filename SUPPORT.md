# Getting support

Label Alchemy is built and maintained by a solo developer. To get help as fast as possible, please use the right channel for what you need.

## Where to go

| Your situation | Best channel |
|----------------|--------------|
| 🐞 **Something is broken or behaves wrong** | [Open a Bug report](../../issues/new?template=bug_report.yml) |
| 💡 **You have an idea or feature request** | [Discussions → Ideas](../../discussions/categories/ideas) (so others can upvote) or [open a Feature request](../../issues/new?template=feature_request.yml) |
| 💬 **A question or "how do I…?"** | [Discussions → Q&A](../../discussions/categories/q-a) |
| 📣 **Want release news** | [Discussions → Announcements](../../discussions/categories/announcements) |
| 📧 **Billing, licensing, or anything private** | Email **support@labelalchemy.dev** |
| 🔒 **A security vulnerability** | Follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) — never a public issue |

## Before you post

1. **Search** [issues](../../issues?q=is%3Aissue) and [discussions](../../discussions) first.
2. Read the **[docs & FAQ](https://docs.labelalchemy.dev)** — most "how do I…" answers live there.
3. Have your **extension version**, **VS Code version**, and **OS** ready (bug reports require them).

## What to expect

- Every issue and discussion is read. Typical response is within a few business days; there is no formal SLA.
- **Paid customers** who email **support@labelalchemy.dev** get priority.
- Feature requests are tracked on the [public roadmap](ROADMAP.md). Upvotes are a strong signal but not a commitment or a delivery date.

## Please never share

API keys, license keys, org credentials, or proprietary source code in public issues or discussions. A small **redacted** snippet is all that's needed to reproduce most bugs. For anything sensitive, email instead.
